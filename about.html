Our Story

22 Years of Making
Events Extraordinary

From a small Miami AV company to South Florida's most trusted full-service event production firm — this is our story.

Founded in Miami

Built on Passion,
Driven by Precision

Serra Industries was founded with a simple belief: every event deserves world-class production. What started as a small audio rental operation in Miami has grown into one of South Florida's most respected full-service event production companies.

Over the past 22 years, we've produced events for Fortune 500 companies, international touring artists, government agencies, nonprofits, and private clients who demand nothing less than perfection. Our reputation was built one event at a time — by showing up early, staying late, and never cutting corners.

Today, Serra Industries operates a fully stocked inventory of professional-grade audio, lighting, video, and staging equipment — backed by a team of experienced technicians, engineers, and creative directors who live and breathe live production.
