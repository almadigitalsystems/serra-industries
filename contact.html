Common Questions

Things People Ask Us

How far in advance should I book?

For major events (concerts, galas, corporate conferences), 2–4 months in advance is ideal. For smaller events, 2–4 weeks is often sufficient. We can accommodate rush bookings when our schedule allows — always worth asking.

Do you travel outside of Miami?

Absolutely. We regularly produce events throughout South Florida (Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach) and travel nationally for larger productions. Travel costs are included in your proposal.

Can you work with our existing AV vendor?

Yes. We're experienced at integrating with house AV systems and coordinating with venue-provided equipment. We'll assess compatibility during our site survey and ensure seamless execution.

What's included in a full production package?

Our full production packages include equipment, transportation, crew (load-in, show, strike), engineering, and project management. You get a single invoice and a single point of contact — no surprises.

Do you do outdoor events?

Yes. Outdoor productions are one of our specialties. We have weather-rated equipment, outdoor-rated speaker systems, and experience with generators, wind-load calculations for staging, and permitting requirements.

Can you support hybrid or livestreamed events?

Absolutely. We have full broadcast and streaming capabilities — multi-camera production, live switching, encoding, and delivery to any streaming platform (Zoom, YouTube, custom RTMP, etc.).
