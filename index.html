What We Do

Full-Spectrum Event Production

We handle every technical element of your event — from the first concept call to the final teardown. No subcontractors, no middlemen — just our expert team.

🎧
Professional Audio

Crystal-clear sound systems engineered for every venue size. Line arrays, monitor mixing, RF coordination, and expert front-of-house engineering.

💡
Lighting Design

Intelligent moving heads, LED fixtures, haze machines, and full show design. We transform any space into an immersive visual experience.

🎬
LED Walls & Video

High-resolution LED walls, seamless multi-screen setups, live switching, broadcast-quality camera systems, and content playback.

Staging & Rigging

Custom staging, trussing, and rigging solutions designed for safety and impact. From small indoor stages to large outdoor festival builds.

Private & Social Events

Our Story

Miami's Most
Trusted AV Partner

Founded in Miami, Serra Industries has grown from a boutique AV rental company into one of South Florida's most respected full-service event production firms. We've produced events for Fortune 500 companies, international touring artists, government agencies, and private clients who demand perfection.

Our secret? We treat every event — large or small — with the same level of care, precision, and passion that built our reputation over two decades.
